Ayurvedic Plant Remedies

Madhumeh

Karela (Momordica charantia)

Upyog kaise karein: Taaze karele ka ras nikaal kar roz subah khaali pet 30-50 ml piyein.

Atirikt jankari: Karela mein aise tatva hote hain jo insulin jaise kaam karte hain aur blood sugar ko kam karte hain.

Methi (Trigonella foenum-graecum)

Upyog kaise karein: 1-2 chammach methi ke beej raat bhar bhigo dein. Subah beej chabaayein aur paani piyein.

Atirikt jankari: Methi mein fiber hota hai jo carbohydrate ke absorption ko dheema karta hai.

Saans ke Rog

Tulsi (Ocimim sanctum)

Upyog kaise karein: 8-10 taazi tulsi ki patte paani mein 5 minute tak ubaal kar adrak aur shahad milaakar din mein do baar piyein.

Atirikt jankari: Tulsi mein anti-inflammatory aur antimicrobial guno hote hain.

Adrak (Zingiber officinale)

Upyog kaise karein: Adrak ko kaddukas karke 10 minute tak paani mein ubaalein. Shahad aur nimbu milaakar din mein 2-3 baar piyein.

Atirikt jankari: Adrak shwas naliyon mein sujan ko kam karta hai.

Ghaav aur Sankraman

Haldi (Curcuma longa)

Upyog kaise karein: Haldi powder ko paani ya narial tel ke saath milaakar lep banaayein aur ghaav par lagaayein.

Atirikt jankari: Haldi mein antiseptic guno hote hain.

Mahilaon ke Mahavari Samasyaayein

Shatavari (Asparagus racemosus)

Upyog kaise karein: 1-2 gram shatavari churna ko doodh ke saath din mein do baar lein.

Atirikt jankari: Shatavari hormone santulan mein madad karta hai.

Aankhon ke Rog

Triphala (Amalaki, Bibhitaki, Haritaki)

Upyog kaise karein: Triphala powder ko raat bhar paani mein bhigo dein aur subah us paani se aankhon ko dho lein.

Atirikt jankari: Triphala aankhon ki sehat sudhaarta hai.

Kide aur Keede

Vidanga (Embelia ribes)

Upyog kaise karein: 1-2 gram vidanga powder ko shahad ya garam paani ke saath lein.

Atirikt jankari: Vidanga pet ke keede nikaalne mein madad karta hai.

Cancer

Curcumin (Haldi se)

Upyog kaise karein: 1 chammach haldi powder ko garam doodh mein milaakar roz piyein.

Atirikt jankari: Curcumin mein cancer rodhi gun hote hain.

Bawaseer

Haritaki (Terminalia chebula)

Upyog kaise karein: 1-2 gram haritaki churna ko garam paani ke saath raat mein lein.

Atirikt jankari: Haritaki pachan sudhaar kar kabz door karta hai, jo bawaseer mein madad karta hai.

Mooli (Raphanus sativus)

Upyog kaise karein: Rozana mooli ka ras piyein ya apne bhojan mein shamil karein.

Atirikt jankari: Mooli sujan aur bleeding ko kam karti hai.

Thyroid Rog

Ashwagandha (Withania somnifera)

Upyog kaise karein: 1-2 gram ashwagandha powder ko garam doodh ke saath rozana lein.

Atirikt jankari: Ashwagandha thyroid hormones ka santulan banaye rakhta hai aur urja badhaata hai.

Guggul (Commiphora wightii)

Upyog kaise karein: 500 mg guggul extract ko din mein do baar paani ke saath lein.

Atirikt jankari: Guggul thyroid function ko badhava deta hai, khaaskar hypothyroidism mein.

Kidney Stone

Upyog kaise karein: Punarnava ki jad ko paani mein ubaal kar uska kadha banaayein aur din mein do baar piyein.

Atirikt jankari: Punarnava ek diuretic hai jo kidney stone ko flush karta hai.

Varuna (Crataeva nurvala)

Upyog kaise karein: 1-2 gram varuna chhaal ka churna paani ke saath din mein do baar lein.

Atirikt jankari: Varuna kidney stone ko gholne mein madad karta hai aur urinary health ko support karta hai.
